Shuttle Logistics
One-way trip — you start at Echo Lake and finish at Tahoe City. Arrange a car shuttle in advance: leave a car at the Tahoe City trailhead (64 Acres Park / Hwy 89 lot), or use WannaRideTahoe.com for a paid shuttle from Barker Pass or Echo Lake. Uber also operates in both Tahoe City and South Lake Tahoe.

Meetup Notes
Friend A: meet at Echo Lake Chalet parking lot morning of June 14. Water taxi available across Echo Lakes (saves ~1.5 miles). Friend B: meet at Barker Pass Trailhead end of Day 4 (June 17 evening). Camp ~0.75 miles in from the trailhead first night together. Barker Pass Rd is off Hwy 89 at Kaspian Campground — follow Blackwood Canyon Rd 7.5 miles to trailhead.
